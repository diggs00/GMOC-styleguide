How to Use Wireless Charging

Vehicles equipped for wireless charging include a special rubberized charging mat located on top of the center console in some vehicles, or, for vehicles equipped with a motorized faceplate, behind the center instrument panel.

To begin charging, make sure:

  • 1

    Your device is paired via Bluetooth. Click here for Bluetooth pairing instructions.

  • 2

    Your charging mat is clear of all other objects.

  • 3

    Your vehicle is turned on, in accessory mode or using Retained Accessory Power.

  • 4

    The phone-charging icon appears near the phone graphic on the vehicle center touch screen. You may need to adjust the position of your phone.
